Donation Guidelines

Help us maintain quality and safety

What to Donate

  • Clean, functional items
  • Textbooks from recent years
  • Electronics in working condition
  • Clothing without stains or tears

What Not to Donate

  • Damaged or broken items
  • Expired food or cosmetics
  • Personal documents
  • Items with safety recalls
