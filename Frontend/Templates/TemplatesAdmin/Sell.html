Pricing Your Items

Get the best value for your items

Textbooks

$20-$150

Pricing Tips:

  • Check current edition value
  • Consider condition honestly
  • Research campus bookstore prices
  • Price 40-60% of retail value

Electronics

$15-$800

Pricing Tips:

  • Check online marketplace values
  • Include original accessories
  • Test all functions first
  • Factor in age and usage

Furniture & Decor

$10-$200

Pricing Tips:

  • Consider pickup logistics
  • Price for quick sale
  • Bundle related items
  • Account for wear and tear

Pro Selling Tips

Maximize your earnings with these strategies

Quality Photos Win

Use natural light, show multiple angles, and highlight key features. Good photos sell items 3x faster.

Timing Matters

List textbooks before semester starts, furniture during move-in/out periods, and winter clothes in fall.

Write Great Descriptions

Be honest about condition, include dimensions, mention any issues, and explain why you're selling.

Price to Sell

Research similar items, price competitively, and be open to reasonable negotiations.
