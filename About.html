About OwlExchange

We're creating a more sustainable and connected campus community where students can easily buy, sell, exchange, and donate items with each other.

Our Mission

Empowering students to live sustainably while building stronger campus communities

Reduce Waste

Keep perfectly good items out of landfills by giving them new life with students who need them.

Save Money

Help students afford college essentials without breaking the bank through affordable secondhand options.

Build Community

Foster meaningful connections between students through shared sustainability values and mutual support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OwlExchange

Is OwlExchange free to use?

Yes! OwlExchange is completely free for students. We don't charge listing fees, transaction fees, or membership costs.

How do I verify my student status?

Simply sign up with your .edu email address. We'll send a verification link to confirm you're a current student.

What if something goes wrong with a transaction?

Our support team is here to help resolve any issues. Use our report system or contact us directly for assistance.

Can I sell items to students at other universities?

Currently, OwlExchange focuses on campus-specific communities to ensure safe, local transactions.

How do you handle disputes between users?

We have a fair resolution process that reviews evidence from both parties and mediates solutions when needed.

Is my personal information secure?

We take privacy seriously and never share your personal information. You control what details to share with other users.
