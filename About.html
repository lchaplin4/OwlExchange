Our Mission

Connecting students for a greener campus.

OwlExchange was founded to bridge the gap between students who need items and those who want to give back. We aim to:

  • Promote sustainability on campus.
  • Reduce waste by reusing and donating items.
  • Create a safe, easy-to-use marketplace.
  • Encourage community engagement.
