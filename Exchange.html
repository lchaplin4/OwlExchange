Pro Trading Tips

Maximize your trading success

Timing is Key

Trade textbooks at semester end, winter gear in spring, and electronics when newer models release.

Bundle for Better Deals

Combine multiple smaller items to match the value of one larger item you want.

Build Your Reputation

Complete trades professionally to build trust and get better offers in the future.

Communicate Clearly

Be upfront about condition, include measurements, and respond promptly to trade inquiries.
